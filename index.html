Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Mumbai's Churchgate district, Gustoso brings the warmth and flavors of Italy to your table. Our carefully curated menu features time-honored Italian recipes prepared with the finest ingredients, sourced both locally and from artisanal producers across Italy. Each dish celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Italy while embracing modern Indian sophistication.
